PWP Milestone 1: Photography Portfolio

Purpose, Audience, Goal

Purpose

By using web development, I intend to give a professional look to one of my favorite hobbies. In addition, I will demonstrate my creativity and how well I can apply the skills I have learned from the bootcamp. In this way, I will be able to demonstrate to tech companies that I am an effective and creative individual.

Audience

It is expected that the audience who will view my website will be a potential customer who is interested in my photography work. It also could be a potential employer who is interested in my work as a web developer. This will allow me to demonstrate my skills in managing and creating a website. There are four target audiences: recruiters, hiring managers, peers, and potential customers.

Goal

A goal of mine is to get into the tech industry and gain as much knowledge as possible so that I can have more opportunities as a web developer. My second option would be to work as a freelance developer or have that skill in order to improve my service.


Persona

Name: Jones Carson

Age: 26

Profession: The individual is a regular businessman who works in accounting and marketing. He possesses effective communication, management and technical skills. To him, everything should be done simply and effectively and he does not like to waste any time getting things done.

TechnologyAn Apple MacBook Air with a M1 chip, a 13.6-inch screen, and a 2560x 1600 resolution screen. The second device is an Apple iPhone 14 with an A15 bionic chip running the latest version of IOS and with a 6.1 inch standard 60Hz OLED screen.

Frustration and Needs: After working overtime and becoming unsatisfied with his work, he is currently looking for a job in social media. He is also interested in people photography and seeks out people photography portfolios in order to evaluate the quality of the images and site.

Goals: Jones wants to hire more photographers with an impressive website portfolio. Accordingly, he is interested in examining the quality of the photographs on the site and how much effort was put into building the site. For the functionality of the website, he wants an easy contact form to show he's interested in doing business.

User Story: As a user, it is critical for me to determine whether this website offers a professional appearance and an easy way to contact the website's creator in order to book a photography shoot.
