About ME

Hello, my name is Aaron Murillo. Currently, I am serving in the army national guard as a military police, and I'm focusing on my education and personal development in the future. In the last few years, I've had a lot of fun traveling and discovering new places. At that moment, I became enamored with photography, so I purchased a camera and began to photograph everything around me. Eventually, that turned into a fun passion for me to take photos and meet new people, and it turned out to be more fun than I could have imagined.

“To photograph is to hold one’s breath, when all faculties converge to capture fleeting reality. It’s at that precise moment that mastering an image becomes a great physical and intellectual joy.” ~Henri Cartier-Bresson
